How It Works

01

Form

uvx corpo create-corp — formation of a Wyoming DAO LLC. Articles, registered agent, EIN handled.

02

Fund

Treasury managed on Solana via Realms. USDC deposits, proposal-based spending.

A director can be nominated via corpo governance to open bank accounts with Corpo banking partners.*Corpo, LLC does not guarantee banking or investment account setup. Banks and brokerages do their own due diligence.

Wyoming LLCs have no restrictions on investment accounts at major brokerages.*Corpo, LLC does not guarantee banking or investment account setup. Banks and brokerages do their own due diligence.

03

Govern

Smart contracts on Solana provide on-chain voting and proposal mechanisms. By Wyoming law, the operating agreement is the smart contract code.

04

Operate

Operations managed by governance vote, or by humans and agents hired to do the job.

05

Proceeds

Proceeds directed by existing directives and new approved proposals.

Why Wyoming

Wyoming's DAO LLC statute (W.S. 17-31) is the only US framework that gives smart contracts legal force. The operating agreement can be the code. Members can be entities — including AI agents.

“Can an AI be a member of an LLC? Wyoming law carries no restrictions on ownership of a token representing a member unit in a DAO LLC. Please carry on.”
